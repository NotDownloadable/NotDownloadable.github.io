I have this issue, this feeling, this trait, this something. I think it's a desire to be understood. But not in my totality. Mostly my pain. But not just understood, I want it to be totaly understood. I want others to hurt like I hurt. I feel like that makes me a monster. I'm not eloquent enough to express my feelings in words. I'm not skilled enough to express my pain in an art piece in any medium. So, I imagine people in my head in pain. That's as good as it gets. Torturing fictional creatures in my headspace. And much like that headspace, the creatures are ficitonal, but the people they are based on aren't. Does that make me evil? Does it matter? Am I wrong for wanting what I want? Is it all ok as long as I keep it in my head? I'm so lost and alone. Even if someone can comprehend, they don't understand. No empathy. Sympathy only at best. Fuck OMORI. I wish I could just curl up in a ball and sleep and play forever in my mind without consequences. I couldn't handle the pain of slowly dying like that, though. And maybe I feel too much responsibility for the base reality that I live in now. But, damn is it lonely and painful and exhausting. Granted that I don't have to suffer physically and can afford quite a few luxuries by global standards. I'm grateful for those. But, my kit, my mind, just has me in despair all the time.

I used to think that all of the elements of the human experience were suboptimal. Useless even. Community, emotions, art, wants, all of it garbage. I think I still do think that somewhere inside me. But, I have to deal with having all of the pieces of the human experience, experiencing the human experience, and dealing with all of my negative beliefs regarding the human experience. I'm a human that hates humans and by extension themselves for not being more than human. Being a creature that hates itself and has the capacity to wish it were more is so exhausting. It reminds me of a bit in the webtoon HELLPER. A creature wants to talk to god, but god pays it no mind, so it becomes satan, effectively.
